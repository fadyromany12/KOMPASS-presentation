KOMPASS

(Konecta Operations, Management & Personnel Self-Service)

Prepared by: Fady Bekhet

1. Executive Summary

The Problem: Operational data is siloed, adherence tracking is manual and reactive, and payroll-impacting errors are frequent. This lack of a single source of truth creates administrative drag and blocks proactive management.

The Solution: KOMPASS, a secure, proprietary web application built on the Google Workspace ecosystem. It centralizes all operational data—from adherence to coaching—into a live command dashboard for managers and a full self-service portal for agents.

The Benefit (ROI): Dramatically reduce administrative overhead, ensure 100% data integrity for payroll and adherence, and empower leadership with real-time, actionable intelligence to drive performance.

2. The Problem: Our Operational Bottlenecks

  • High-Cost Manual Reporting: Adherence and payroll reports are time-consuming, resource-intensive, and prone to human error.
  • Lack of Real-Time Visibility: Managers are blind to live team status (breaks, lates, aux states), preventing proactive floor management.
  • Data Integrity & Payroll Risk: Manual data entry for schedules and leave directly leads to costly payroll disputes and skewed performance metrics.
  • Fragmented Approval Workflows: Leave, schedule, and hierarchy changes are lost in untraceable email chains, creating an accountability "black hole."
  • Agent Disempowerment: Agents have no self-service access to their schedules, leave balances, or coaching history, creating constant dependency.

3. The Solution: The KOMPASS Platform

KOMPASS replaces fragmented, manual workflows with a single, secure, and automated "source of truth."

4. Core Features: A 360° Solution

KOMPASS delivers a tailored, permission-based experience for every role in our operation.

AGENT SELF-SERVICE PORTAL

A secure, single destination for agents to manage their schedule, leave, and performance, dramatically reducing administrative dependency.

MANAGER COMMAND CENTER

A live dashboard and suite of powerful admin tools for real-time team management, coaching, and automated reporting.

SYSTEM ADMINISTRATION HUB

A top-level interface for secure user onboarding, system-wide configuration, and content management.

Features for All Users (Agents)

  • Digital Punch Clock: Simple, secure interface for all time-tracking (Login, Breaks, Lunch, Logout).
  • Auxiliary State Management: Accurately log all non-production states (Coaching, Meeting, Personal) for precise adherence reporting.
  • My Schedule & Balances: 24/7 self-service access to view upcoming schedules and real-time leave balances.
  • Automated Leave Requests: Submit, track, and review the status of all leave requests through a single portal.
  • Coaching & Acknowledgement: A central hub to review all historical coaching sessions and provide digital acknowledgement.
  • Live Announcements: Receive critical, real-time broadcasts from system administrators directly on the punch clock page.

Features for Managers (Admins)

  • Live Team Dashboard: A "Mission Control" view of the entire team's live status, including adherence deviations and pending requests, filterable by hierarchy.
  • Reporting Line Management: A full workflow to manage team structures, including submitting and approving agent movements between supervisors.
  • Centralized Approval Queues: A dedicated, auditable queue to approve or deny all subordinate leave and movement requests.
  • Advanced Schedule Editor: Manually edit schedules for individuals or bulk-upload entire team schedules via CSV.
  • Powerful Admin Tools: Generate detailed adherence reports, perform manual balance adjustments, and correct punch times.
  • Dynamic Coaching Platform: Submit detailed, template-driven coaching sessions for all subordinates and track follow-up actions.

Features for System Admins (Superadmins)

  • Secure User Registration Gateway: Approve or deny new, auto-registered users and securely assign them to their correct supervisor in the hierarchy.
  • Dynamic Coaching Template Builder: Full CRUD interface to create, edit, and manage the dynamic coaching templates used by all managers.
  • System-Wide Broadcast Admin: Create, edit, and broadcast live announcements to all active users on the platform.
  • Role & Permission Management: A secure queue to review and approve/deny role upgrade requests (e.g., Agent to Admin) from existing users.
