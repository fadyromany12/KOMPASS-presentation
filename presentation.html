KOMPASS

(Konecta Operations, Management & Personnel Self-Service)

Prepared by: Fady Bekhet

1. Executive Summary

The Problem: Outdated, error-prone manual processes are draining resources, eroding our bottom line, and providing zero real-time operational visibility.

The Solution: KOMPASS, a proprietary Google Workspace tool, digitizes and revolutionizes these workflows, providing a live command dashboard for managers and total self-service for agents.

The Benefit (ROI): KOMPASS will reclaim thousands of administrative hours, deliver 100% data integrity for payroll, and transform managers from reactive reporters to proactive leaders.

2. The Problem: Our Operational Bottlenecks

  • The Daily Grind: Manual adherence reports are labor-intensive and obsolete by the time they're finished.
  • The 'Black Box' Problem: Managers are blind to real-time team status (breaks, lates, coaching).
  • The High Cost of 'Oops': Manual entry errors directly impact payroll, adherence metrics, and employee morale.
  • The Approval Logjam: Leave and schedule changes are lost in email chains, lacking tracking and accountability.
  • The Disempowered Agent: Agents are 100% dependent on managers for basic info (schedules, balances, history).

3. The Solution: The KOMPASS Platform

KOMPASS replaces fragmented, manual workflows with a single, secure, and automated "source of truth."

THE OLD WAY (REACTIVE)

  • Manual spreadsheets & data entry
  • Email chains for approvals
  • No live data (24h+ delay)
  • Constant data errors & disputes
  • Payroll corrections
  • Reactive management

THE KOMPASS WAY (PROACTIVE)

  • Centralized, secure database
  • Automated approval workflows
  • Live, auto-refreshing dashboard
  • 100% data integrity, zero errors
  • Payroll accuracy
  • Proactive, real-time leadership

4. Core Features: A 360Â° Solution

KOMPASS delivers a tailored, permission-based experience for every role in our operation.

FOR AGENTS

A powerful self-service portal that empowers employees and reduces supervisor dependency.

FOR MANAGERS

A real-time command center and robust admin tools to manage teams proactively.

FOR SUPERADMINS

Total system-wide configuration, user onboarding, and content management.

Features for All Users (Agents)

  • One-Click Punch Clock: Simple, intuitive interface for Login, Logout, Breaks, and Lunch.
  • Auxiliary Code Tracking: Accurately log non-adherence time (Coaching, Meeting, Personal).
  • My Schedule Portal: View their official, upcoming 7-day schedule 24/7.
  • My Leave Module: View real-time balances and submit new leave requests in seconds.
  • Coaching Hub: Review all past coaching sessions and digitally **acknowledge** them.
  • Live Announcement Banner: See real-time broadcasts from admins on the main page.

Features for Managers (Admins)

  • Live Team Dashboard: Auto-refreshing "Mission Control" panel of all team members' current statuses.
  • Hierarchy Management: View and manage team structures; approve agent-to-team movements.
  • Leave Approval Queue: A dedicated queue to approve or deny subordinate leave requests.
  • Advanced Schedule Editor: Manually edit schedules or bulk-upload via CSV.
  • Reporting & Admin Tools: Generate CSV history reports, adjust balances, and submit leave on behalf of agents.
  • Team Coaching Module: Submit and track coaching sessions for all subordinates.

Features for System Admins (Superadmins)

  • User Registration Gateway: Secure approval queue to activate new users and assign them to a supervisor.
  • Dynamic Coaching Admin: Full CRUD interface to build and manage the dynamic coaching templates used by all managers.
  • Broadcast Admin: Full CRUD interface to create, edit, and broadcast live announcements to all users.

5. Key Business Benefits (The "Why")
