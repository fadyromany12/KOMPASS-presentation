Business Case & Proposal

KOMPASS

(Konecta Operations, Management & Personnel Self-Service)

Prepared by: Fady Bekhet

1. Executive Summary

The Problem: Manual, error-prone processes for tracking adherence, leave, and schedules are costing time and money, while providing zero real-time visibility.

The Solution: KOMPASS, a secure Google Workspace tool, centralizes and automates these tasks, providing a live dashboard for managers and self-service for agents.

The Benefit (ROI): Immediately saves hundreds of admin hours, drastically improves data accuracy for payroll, and empowers managers to lead proactively.

2. The Problem: Current Inefficiencies

  • Manual Adherence Tracking: Supervisors spend hours manually compiling reports—a reactive, not proactive, process.
  • Lack of Real-Time Visibility: No way to see who is on break, late, or in coaching *right now*.
  • Data & Payroll Errors: Manual data entry leads to frequent mistakes, payroll discrepancies, and wasted time on corrections.
  • Inefficient Leave Management: Disconnected emails and spreadsheets make tracking balances and approvals a nightmare.
  • Poor Employee Experience: Agents have no self-service for schedules, leave, or performance, creating constant dependency on managers.

3. The Solution: The KOMPASS Platform

KOMPASS replaces fragmented, manual workflows with a single, secure, and automated "source of truth" built on our existing Google environment.

BEFORE KOMPASS

  • Manual spreadsheets
  • Email chains for leave
  • No live data
  • Adherence reports are 24h late
  • Constant data errors
  • Agent dependency on manager

AFTER KOMPASS

  • Centralized database
  • Automated approval workflows
  • Live, auto-refreshing dashboard
  • Real-time adherence alerts
  • Guaranteed data integrity
  • Full agent self-service

4. Core Features: A 360° Solution

KOMPASS provides distinct, permission-based features for every role in the company.

FOR AGENTS

Self-service tools that empower employees and reduce supervisor dependency.

FOR MANAGERS

Real-time dashboards and admin tools to manage teams proactively.

FOR SUPERADMINS

System-wide configuration and high-level user management.

Features for All Users (Agents)

  • Punch Clock: Simple interface for Login, Logout, Breaks, and Lunch.
  • Auxiliary Codes: Track non-adherence time (Coaching, Meeting, Personal).
  • My Schedule: View upcoming 7-day schedule.
  • My Leave Module: View real-time balances and submit new leave requests.
  • Coaching History: Review all past coaching sessions and digitally acknowledge them.
  • Live Announcements: See real-time broadcasts from admins on the main page.

Features for Managers (Admins)

  • Live Team Dashboard: Auto-refreshing panel of all team members' current statuses.
  • Hierarchy Management: View and manage team structures; approve agent-to-team movements.
  • Leave Approvals: A dedicated queue to approve or deny subordinate leave requests.
  • Schedule Editor: Manually edit schedules or bulk-upload via CSV.
  • Reporting & Admin Tools: Generate CSV history reports, adjust balances, and submit leave on behalf of agents.
  • Team Coaching: Submit and track coaching sessions for all subordinates.

Features for System Admins (Superadmins)

  • User Registration Admin: Approval queue to activate new users and assign them to a supervisor.
  • Coaching Admin: Full CRUD interface to build and manage the dynamic coaching templates used by all managers.
  • Announcements Admin: Full CRUD interface to create, edit, and broadcast live announcements to all users.

5. Key Business Benefits (ROI)

Massive Time Savings

Automates adherence, leave, and reporting, freeing supervisors for value-add tasks like coaching.

Data Integrity & Accuracy

Eliminates manual entry errors, providing a single source of truth for adherence and payroll.

Proactive Management

Live, auto-refreshing dashboard allows managers to see and address issues *as they happen*, not 24 hours later.

Employee Empowerment

Self-service for leave, schedules, and coaching reduces supervisor dependency and improves transparency.

Standardized Processes

Enforces a single, compliant workflow for all requests, changes, and coaching documentation.

Secure & Scalable

Built on our Google Workspace. No new logins, no extra infrastructure costs. Infinitely scalable.
